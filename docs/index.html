Container

The container element simple centers your content horizontally. It's often used as a child element of a section and wraps around columns. 

<div class="container">
    <div class="columns">
        <div class="column">1</div>
        <div class="column">2</div>
        <div class="column">3</div>
        <div class="column">4</div>
        <div class="column">5</div>
    </div>
</div>
